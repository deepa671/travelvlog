About the Website

The website features:

Home is the main page with an overview of the Travel Journal, Login is the page for registered users to log into their accounts, Signup is a age for new users to create an account, To Do List allows users to manage their travel plans and tasks, Journal is used to write and store travel experiences, Travel Album is used to store and display travel photos, Logout helps to log out from the current session.
